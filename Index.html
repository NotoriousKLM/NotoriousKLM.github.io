Frequently Asked Questions

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Reach out to our customer support team.

Do you offer gluten-free options?
Yes, we offer gluten-free cookies upon request. Unfortunately there will be an additional charge.
Can I customize my cookie order?
Yes, we offer customization options for some cookie orders. Please contact us for more information.
Do you deliver?
Yes, we offer delivery services for an additional fee. Please check our delivery area and fees on our website.
What is your return policy?
We do not accept returns on our made-to-order cookies. However, if you are unsatisfied with your order, please contact us and we will do our best to resolve the issue.
How far in advance should I place my order?
We recommend placing your order at least 3 days in advance to ensure availability. However, we may be able to accommodate last-minute orders depending on our schedule.
