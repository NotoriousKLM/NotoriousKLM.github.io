Frequently Asked Questions

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Reach out to our customer support team.

Absolutely! Each order is made-to-order to fit your needs. Contact us for more details.
Do you deliver?
Yes, delivery is available for an additional fee. Check our website for delivery areas and rates.

Since each cookie is custom-made, we do not accept returns. However, please contact us if there’s an issue with your order.
